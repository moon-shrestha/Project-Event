WHO WE ARE



From concept to execution, Event planner works with your vision and makes it more impactful than you could have imagined. For over 10 years, Event planner has worked to design, plan, coordinate and produce events that transform the ordinary to the truly unforgettable for clients and their guests.

As your full service Event Planner, we are committed to stunning design and the ultimate attention to detail to ensure an event that truly reflects who you are.

Our Experience

We began our career in event production 19 years ago, while organizing presentations for Sony Pictures Entertainment. We acted as a single team and learned live production from the ground-up. Within a year, our responsibilities expanded to include securing talent and prime exhibit space, overseeing lighting and LED video wall installations, set design and decor, negotiating live satellite feeds in major hotels and producing live game shows throughout the country.
